---
title: introduction

fireball is an agentic layer for web3 and crypto-enabled games. instead of grinding through every action yourself, fireball lets you step up a level: your agents execute the strategy while you focus on the bigger picture. this frees up your time for deeper analysis, smarter decisions, and actually enjoying the game as a strategist rather than a button-presser.

---

fireball's deepest integration today is with  [gigaverse](https://gigaverse.io). your agents can run dungeons, go fishing, collect yield from various nfts, and manage daily routines, all in a new agentic-era style of gameplay. what used to take hours of manual repetition now runs in the background while you plan your next move. 


## features

- **dungeons** — AI agents play dungeon combat on your behalf across Normal and Underhaul difficulty types
- **fishing** — Automated or manual fishing with an AI hint system
- **services** — Background workers for Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, and Hatchery
- **world & crafting** — Alchemy, Workbench, Cooking, Merchant, and more
- **pets & eggs** — Feed pets, hatch eggs, and manage your collection
- **roms** — Manage and claim ROM rewards
- **quests** — Complete quests and earn Spark rewards
- **inventory** — Browse and filter your items with live pricing data
- **jobs** — Monitor all active and completed automation runs
- **leaderboards** — See how you stack up against other players
