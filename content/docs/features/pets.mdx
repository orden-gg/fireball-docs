title: Pets
description: Manage and feed your hatched pet companions
---

The Pets page shows all your hatched pets. Feed hungry pets, track their stats, and filter your collection.

## Filters

### Rarity

Filter by: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Relic, Giga

### Faction

Filter by faction membership, including "No Faction" for unaffiliated pets.

### Egg Type

Filter by origin: Inaugural Steed, Silver ROM, Gold ROM, Void ROM, or Giga ROM.

### Gender

Filter by: Male or Female

### Hungry

A quick-toggle checkbox at the top shows only hungry pets with a count badge. When active, pets are sorted by hunger level (hungriest first) so you can prioritize feeding.

## Feeding Pets

Click a hungry pet to open the **feed overlay** — a grid showing all available food items from your inventory.

Each food item displays:

- Your current supply (available / total)
- Food effects as tooltips, including:
  - **Reduce Pet Hunger** — Decreases hunger level
  - **Prevent Pet Hunger Loss** — Adds hunger buffer
  - **Increase Pet Output** — Boosts production
  - **Increase High Tier Chance** — Improves rare outcome probability

Disabled food (insufficient supply) appears grayed out.

## Sorting

- **Default** — Sorted by rarity (highest first)
- **Hungry filter active** — Sorted by hunger level (hungriest first)
