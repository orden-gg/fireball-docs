The Pets page is where you manage your hatched pets and unhatched eggs. Feed hungry pets, track hatching progress, and filter through your collection.

## Views

Toggle between three views at the top of the page:

| View     | Shows                       |
| -------- | --------------------------- |
| **All**  | Both pets and eggs together |
| **Pets** | Only hatched pets           |
| **Eggs** | Only unhatched eggs         |

## Filters

The Pets page has extensive filtering options to help you find exactly what you're looking for:

### Rarity (Pets only)

Filter by: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Relic, Giga

### Faction (Pets only)

Filter by faction membership, including "No Faction" for unaffiliated pets.

### Egg Type

Filter by origin: Inaugural Steed, Silver ROM, Gold ROM, Void ROM, or Giga ROM. This works for both pet and egg views.

### Gender (Pets only)

Filter by: Male or Female

### Hatchery Status (Eggs only)

Filter eggs by whether they're currently in the hatchery or not.

### Hungry (Pets only)

A quick-toggle checkbox at the top shows only hungry pets with a count badge. When active, pets are sorted by hunger level (hungriest first) so you can prioritize feeding.

## Feeding Pets

Click a hungry pet to open the **feed overlay** — a grid showing all available food items from your inventory.

Each food item displays:

- Your current supply (available / total)
- Food effects as tooltips, including:
  - **Reduce Pet Hunger** — Decreases hunger level
  - **Prevent Pet Hunger Loss** — Adds hunger buffer
  - **Increase Pet Output** — Boosts production
  - **Increase High Tier Chance** — Improves rare outcome probability

Disabled food (insufficient supply) appears grayed out.

## Egg Hatching

Eggs show their hatching progress:

| Status             | Display                        |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------ |
| **Not started**    | No progress bar                |
| **In hatchery**    | Progress percentage (0–100%)   |
| **Ready to hatch** | Full progress, ready to reveal |

Eggs are sorted by priority: ready to hatch first, then by progress (highest first), then unstarted eggs last.

You can automate egg management with the [Hatchery service](/features/services#hatchery), which maintains temperature, comfort, and fate levels automatically.

## Sorting

- **Default** — Hatched pets first (sorted by rarity, highest first), then eggs
- **Hungry filter active** — Pets sorted by hunger level (hungriest first)
- **Eggs** — Sorted by: Ready to hatch → In hatchery (by progress) → Not started
