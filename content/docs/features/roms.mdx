ROMs are digital assets in Gigaverse that produce resources over time — energy, shards, dust, and GIGUS dust. The ROMs page lets you manage your collection, view production stats, and claim accumulated resources.

## ROM Grid

Your ROMs are displayed as cards with key information at a glance. The header shows your total count: **N ROMs (M boosted)** — where "boosted" refers to child ROMs that inherit properties from parent ROMs.

## ROM Tiers

ROMs come in four tiers, each with different production rates and value:

| Tier       | Color  |
| ---------- | ------ |
| **Silver** | Gray   |
| **Gold**   | Yellow |
| **Void**   | Pink   |
| **Giga**   | Red    |

## Filters

| Filter          | Options                                               |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tier**        | Toggle Silver, Gold, Void, and/or Giga (multi-select) |
| **Faction**     | Dropdown with faction icons and "All" option          |
| **Memory Sort** | Sort by memory value: ascending, descending, or none  |
| **Reset**       | Clear all active filters                              |

## ROM Core

The ROM Core section is a two-card dashboard at the top of the page:

### Energy Card

Shows your energy production and reserves:

- **Total Energy** — Current / Maximum capacity
- **Default Energy** — Base energy from your account
- **ROM Energy** — Additional energy from ROMs
- **Time period toggle** — View production rates for Day, Week, or Month
- **Convert Energy** — Button to convert excess energy to GIGUS Dust (shows count of convertible ROMs)

### Actions Card

- **ROM Stats Total** — Aggregate production stats across all ROMs
- **Claim Resources** — Button to manually claim accumulated resources from all eligible ROMs (shows count of claimable ROMs)

## Automated ROM Management

Instead of manually claiming resources, you can subscribe to the [ROM Claimer service](/features/services#rom-claimer) to automatically claim when ROMs reach 90% capacity. The service also supports converting excess energy to GIGUS Dust with a configurable energy threshold.

## Child ROMs

Child (boosted) ROMs are sorted to the bottom of the grid and excluded from aggregate statistics. They're tracked separately in the header count.
