## Getting Started

### What is Fireball?

Fireball is **the** go-to complementary game client, parallel meta-game and autobattler for Gigaverse — helping you save precious time, focus on strategy, and engage in this parallel ecosystem.

### Is Fireball free to use?

You can try Fireball free for 5 days by claiming the **Scroll of Noob** from the Quests page. After that, you'll need to be under the effects of Giga-Juice and purchase [Sparks](/guides/sparks) to continue.

### How do I get started?

1. Create an account at [gigaverse.io](https://gigaverse.io)
2. Sign in to Fireball with the same Abstract wallet
3. Claim the Scroll of Noob for your 5-day free trial
4. Get Giga-Juiced
5. Purchase Sparks after the trial ends

See the [Introduction](/) for the full setup walkthrough.

## Sparks

### What are Sparks?

Sparks are Fireball's credit system. Every automated action — dungeon runs, fishing sessions, services, and hints — consumes Sparks. They're purchased with ETH on the [Sparks page](/guides/sparks).

### Are Sparks a token?

No. Sparks are not, and never will be, a cryptocurrency token. They are credits within the Fireball platform.

### How many Sparks do I need?

Use the cost calculator on the Sparks page — select your game type (Dungeon or Fishing) and time range (Day, Week, Month) to get a personalized estimate based on your energy and ROMs.

### What's the 30-day limit?

Each account can purchase a maximum of 20,000 Sparks per 30-day rolling period. This is enforced by the smart contract.

## Account & Access

### What is the message I'm signing to login?

The message is the same one Gigaverse requires. It allows Fireball to make in-game moves on your behalf. It does **NOT** grant access to your wallet funds or allow any blockchain transactions.

### Can I use multiple accounts?

Yes. If you have multiple Gigaverse accounts under one wallet, use the **account switcher** in the dashboard sidebar to swap between them.

### What is Giga-Juice?

Giga-Juice is an effect in Gigaverse that's required to access Fireball's full features. You can check your Giga-Juice status and expiration in the Fireball sidebar.

### What are the Scrolls?

- **Scroll of Noob** — 5-day free trial for dungeon and fishing runs
- **Scroll of Fire** — Permanent free access to games and all [Services](/features/services) (Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, Hatchery)

## Using Fireball

### Do I need to keep my browser open?

No. Fireball runs everything on its servers. You can close your browser, turn off your PC, and come back later to check results.

### What agent AI model should I use for dungeons?

Fireball's dungeon agent AI has three presets:

- **Defensive** — Safer, prioritizes survival
- **Balanced** — Good default for most situations
- **Offensive** — Faster but riskier

Start with Balanced and adjust based on your win rate.

### What's the difference between Agent and Manual fishing?

- **Agent** — Fireball's agent AI plays automatically. Supports batch sessions.
- **Manual** — You play each card yourself. Limited to 1 session, but you get agent AI hints with confidence levels.

### What are Services?

Services are background automations that run on a schedule:

- **Fish Seller** — Sells fish for seaweed
- **ROM Claimer** — Claims ROM resources at 90% capacity
- **Loot Collector** — Breaks pots and chests
- **Hatchery** — Maintains egg hatching conditions

See the full [Services guide](/features/services).

### Can I install Fireball as an app?

Yes — Fireball is a Progressive Web App. Install it from your browser for quick access and push notifications where supported. See [Installing the App](/guides/pwa-install).
