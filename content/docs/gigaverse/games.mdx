description: Overview of the games available in Gigaverse and how Fireball automates them.
---

Gigaverse has three game modes. Fireball can automate all of them.

## Dungeons

A combat game where you battle through 16 rooms of enemies to earn loot. Fireball's AI agent handles every combat decision — choose a playstyle preset (Defensive, Balanced, or Offensive), set the number of runs, and walk away.

Two dungeon types are available:

| Type          | Rooms | Energy | Daily Limit |
| ------------- | ----- | ------ | ----------- |
| **Normal**    | 16    | 40     | 10 (12 juiced) |
| **Underhaul** | 16    | 40     | 8 (9 juiced)   |

## Fishing

A card-based game where you battle a fish to reel it in. Run it fully automated with Agent mode, or play manually with AI-powered hints.

Three fishing types are available:

| Type       | Energy | Daily Limit       |
| ---------- | ------ | ----------------- |
| **Small**  | 12     | 10 (20 juiced)    |
| **Normal** | 16     | 10 (20 juiced)    |
| **Big**    | 20     | 10 (20 juiced)    |

## Conquest

A territory control strategy game played on a hexagonal map. Factions compete for tiles across timed ticks. Fireball provides a full map viewer with leaderboards, faction management, and tick history. Conquest is not automated — it's a player-driven strategy mode.
