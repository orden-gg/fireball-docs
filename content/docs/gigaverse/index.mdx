title: Overview
description: What is Gigaverse — the blockchain RPG that Fireball is built for.
---

Gigaverse is a blockchain RPG built by GLHF on the [Abstract](https://abs.xyz) chain. It's a pixel art crypto RPG focused on accessibility and risk-reward mechanics — dungeons, fishing, territory conquest, and a player-driven economy.

Fireball is a companion client for Gigaverse. It doesn't replace the game — it automates repetitive actions and gives you better tooling on top of it.

For full game documentation, visit the [official Gigaverse docs](https://glhfers.gitbook.io/gigaverse).
